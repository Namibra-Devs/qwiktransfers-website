— FAQ

Questions, answered.

How long does a transfer to Ghana take?
Most mobile money transfers (MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo) arrive in under 60 seconds. Bank deposits to GCB, Ecobank, Fidelity and others typically arrive within 10 minutes during banking hours.
How much does it cost to send money?
QwikTransfer charges zero transfer fees — always. You only pay through the exchange rate. What you see is exactly what your family receives, with no hidden charges, no flat fees, and no FX margin tricks. Ever.
Is QwikTransfer safe and regulated?
Yes. We're a FINTRAC-registered Money Services Business in Canada and licensed by the Bank of Ghana for inbound remittances. All transfers are protected by 256-bit encryption.
What payment methods can I use?
You can pay with Interac e-Transfer, Canadian debit cards, credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), or directly from your Canadian bank account. Interac is the cheapest and fastest option.
Can I send money to a bank or only mobile money?
Both. Send to any major Ghanaian bank (GCB, Ecobank, Fidelity, Stanbic, Zenith, and 20+ more), to MTN MoMo / Vodafone Cash / AirtelTigo Money, or for cash pickup at over 1,500 locations across Ghana.
What's the maximum amount I can send?
Verified accounts can send up to $25,000 CAD per transaction and $50,000 per month. Need to send more? Contact our team and we'll set up a higher limit for you.
