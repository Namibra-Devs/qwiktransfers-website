Our Story

Empowering global connections through seamless transfers.

We built QwikTransfer because we experienced the frustration of sending money back home. We believe remittance should be fast, secure, and completely transparent.

Built by Ghanaians,
for Ghanaians.

Our journey started with a simple question: Why does it cost so much and take so long to send money to the people we love?

Today, QwikTransfer is a fully regulated financial institution operating under FINTRAC in Canada and licensed by the Bank of Ghana. We are a dedicated team of engineers, financial experts, and support staff working around the clock.
